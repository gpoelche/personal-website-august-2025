Intro

Greetings, my name is George. I’m a Pittsburgh-based Full-Stack Development student at Arizona State University. I enjoy creating experiences on the web that are accessible, clean in design and enjoyable in use. I have a strong interest in the development of web apps, tools or entertainment experiences.

I am also currently a lab technician at the Bodega Film Lab, Pittsburgh’s greatest resource for film development. My responsibilities range from developing all black and white film in-house by hand, machine maintenance and upkeep, film scanning and color correction, as well as community event planning.

Photography

Film photography has become an essential source of creative expression in my life for the past three years. I began shooting on a secondhand Minolta SRT 201 capturing photos of my life in Pittsburgh in 2023, primarily in black and white. Street photography is a major focus within my work, but I have become more explorative of more subjects over time. I now shoot mostly medium format with my Mamiya 645, balanced more evenly with both color and black and white.
